# Client Discovery Questions — Jellies / GE Solutions Website

Based on a full code analysis, the website is built for an international export/trade business currently named "Global Export Solutions (GE Solutions)". The project codename is "Jellies". Below are all questions needed before the site can be completed and launched.

---

## 1. Branding & Identity

- 1.1 What is the official company name? (Currently shows "Global Export Solutions" / "GE Solutions")
- 1.2 Do you have a logo (SVG/PNG)? Please share both light and dark versions.
- 1.3 Do you have a favicon for the browser tab?
- 1.4 What are the brand colors? (Currently: dark navy + amber/gold)
- 1.5 Is the current font set fine? (Inter + Outfit from Google Fonts)
- 1.6 What is the company tagline/slogan? (Currently: "Connecting Markets, Delivering Trust.")

---

## 2. Business & Content

- 2.1 What year was the company founded? (Currently shows: 2010)
- 2.2 How many countries do you serve? (Currently: 45+)
- 2.3 How many tons have been exported? (Currently: 10,000+)
- 2.4 How many clients do you have? (Currently: 500+)
- 2.5 Do you have certifications? (e.g. ISO 9001 — currently listed)
- 2.6 What are your real annual shipments and on-time accuracy? (Currently: 1500+ / 98%)
- 2.7 Do you want to keep the Mission / Heritage / Integrity text, or do you have your own About page content?
- 2.8 What company photos do you have? (warehouses, team, shipments, products) — currently using stock images

---

## 3. Products

- 3.1 What product categories do you sell? (Currently: Industrial, Agriculture, Chemicals, Electronics, Textiles)
- 3.2 Please provide a product list with: name, category, short description, full description, specifications, and images.
- 3.3 Should product prices be visible to visitors, or is it enquiry-based only?
- 3.4 Do you want a minimum order quantity (MOQ) field per product?

---

## 4. Services

- 4.1 What services do you offer? (Currently placeholder: Product Sourcing, Global Supply Chain, Export Compliance)
- 4.2 For each service, provide: title, short description, full description, and image.
- 4.3 Should services have individual detail pages, or just a contact/enquiry link?

---

## 5. Blog

- 5.1 Do you plan to publish a blog? (Blog is fully built in the admin panel)
- 5.2 Do you have initial blog posts to start with?
- 5.3 Who will write and manage blog content going forward?

---

## 6. Contact & Communication

- 6.1 What is the company admin email? (Used for enquiry notifications)
- 6.2 What is the customer-facing email? (Shown in footer/contact page)
- 6.3 What is the phone number (with country code)?
- 6.4 What is the WhatsApp number? (Currently a placeholder in the hero button)
- 6.5 What is the company address?
- 6.6 Do you have a Google Maps embed URL for the contact/location?
- 6.7 What are your social media links? (Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X)

---

## 7. Gallery

- 7.1 Do you want a photo/video gallery page? (The route is scaffolded but not yet designed)
- 7.2 If yes, what type of content? (Products, warehouses, shipments, events, team?)
- 7.3 Do you have existing media files to populate it?

---

## 8. Chatbot (AI Assistant)

- 8.1 Do you want the AI chatbot widget active on the live site? (It is already built)
- 8.2 If yes, should it use OpenAI (ChatGPT) or Google Gemini?
- 8.3 What topics/questions should the chatbot handle?
- 8.4 What should the chatbot persona be called? (e.g. "GE Trade Assistant")

---

## 9. Admin Panel & Accounts

- 9.1 What email address(es) should have admin dashboard access?
- 9.2 Should there be multiple admin users or just one?
- 9.3 Do you want customer login/register on the public site, or is it enquiry-only?

---

## 10. SEO & Domain

- 10.1 What is the domain name? (Currently placeholder: globalexsolutions.com)
- 10.2 What country/region is your primary target market?
- 10.3 Are there specific keywords you want to rank for on Google?

---

## 11. Launch & Deployment

- 11.1 Where should the site be hosted? (Vercel, Firebase Hosting, or custom server?)
- 11.2 Do you have a Firebase project ready, or should we create one?
- 11.3 What is the target launch date?
- 11.4 Are there any competitor/reference websites you like the look of?
